Positions ♠

∘There are four positions in football: Striker, Midfielder, Defender and Goalkeeper. Goalkeepers have the role to keep the ball away from the net. Defenders have the role of not letting the opponent get close or take a shot at the net. Midfielders are those controlling the play during the game and helping both Strikers and Defenders to complete their task. Strikers are the ones who have to try scoring into the opposing net.

∘Each team are required to have a maximum of 1 goalkeeper on the field. The rest of the positions are decided by the coach/manager of the team. For the defense the more common positions are known as Center Back, Left Back, Full Back and Right Back. For the midfield, the common positions are Center Mid, Right Mid, Left Mid, Center Attacking Mid and Center Defensive Mid. For the strikers, there are Left Wingers, Right Wingers, Center Forwards and Striker itself.
